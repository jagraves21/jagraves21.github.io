Grants

Sheikh Ghafoor has supervised numerous scientific research grants during his career (16 as the PI and 8 as the Co-PI). Project funds have totaled over $7 million with Tennessee Tech receiving over $4 million. In recent times, Sheikh's research has been supported by the National Science Foundation (NSF), the National Security Agency (NSA), and Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL).
